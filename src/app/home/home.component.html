We're here to help

Barkat- “A step towards humanity." Our major objective as a welfare association is to identify the multi facet problems that our nation is facing and persistently sensitising the youth towards it. We vision to facilitate the innate humanity within each individual and channelize it into compassionate and selfless service towards mankind. We thoroughly believe in and there by implement the principle of sewa, and endeavour to inculcate it within the youth.

We at Barkat Welfare Society are obliged to receive the certification from the esteemed NGO Association of India for our work and dedication towards the society's upliftment. We pledge to work with same dedication and efforts in future and will provide a helping hand wherever it'll be possible.

Our Aim

Our work aims is to break the vicious cycle of poverty and social isolation and to restore hope for a better future. We believe that every person has the right to access resources and opportunities in order to live and develop with dignity and to become an active and contributing member of our society.

We also believe that we should provide our environment in return of what we take, so we are aiming to plant 10,000 plants in one month in our current drive and will be contributing to the environment ahead in future in whatever way we can.
