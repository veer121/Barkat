Campaigns " A life lived for others is a life well lived"
Currently we are working on the MEGA PLANTATION DRIVE in which we aim to plant 10, 000 plants in one month. With this we can conserve the environment keeping in consideration of the social distancing norms and still can help the nature to heal. Also, we're doing SUNDAY DRIVES in which we feeds animals, workship the GOD and do tasks that motivates an individual to achieve something great in his/her lives.
Educational Aid Barkat has constantly endeavored to deliver basic education to that stratum of the society which has been obscure in the eyes of general public. Education occupies a gigantic layer at the base of our vision of an ideally prosperous society. We aspire to make resourceful assets that are a lot less vulnerable to poverty and unemployment.
Medical Aid Medical treatments often cost a huge amount of money which is unaffordable to the underprivileged sections. Barkat has made numerous efforts at the grass root level to bride in this gap to develop a less elusive and more approachable healthcare system for everyone.
Plantation Drives In lieu with the Sustainable Development Goals of UNDP; we have taken it upon ourselves to make a greener and healthy ecosystem around us. Even amidst a pandemic, we made plantation drives a possibility by coordinating with volunteers all over India to plant 10,000 saplings and seeds. Restoration and creation of a sustainable abode to undo human negligence is one of the central ideas of Barkat.
Mass Distributions Barkat has believed in sharing the privilege that god almighty has bestowed upon us with those who seek help. Basic objects of sustenance like groceries, clothes and blankets are distributed regularly to always uphold this belief that everyone deserves two square meals and protection from extremities.
Help during natural disasters As disasters, earthquakes and floods become a regular ordeal; Barkat makes sure that we can reduce and absorb the impact of the same by mobilizing available resources and directing them towards the worst affected area of the disaster. Impromptu action and immediate relief becomes our main focus in times of desperate trouble.
Educational seminars Youth has been the driving force of Barkat. Training and preparing students for future is an avenue where we have expanded gracefully over the years. Organizing educational and training seminars for the students of various institutions in order to prepare them for future is another aspect of our goals.
Driven by a passion to serve the needy Barkat welfare society aims to alleviate poverty by providing education, healthcare, and community development to the neediest communities across India. Several Activities have been organized by Barkat in order to spread awareness about the socio - economic issues prevailing in the society
